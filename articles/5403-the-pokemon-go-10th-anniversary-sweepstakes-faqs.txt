* **When is the Pokémon GO 10**  **th**  **Anniversary Sweepstakes taking place?**

* Starts March 10, 2026 at 12:00:00 a.m. Pacific Daylight Saving Time (“ **PDT** ”) and ends April 30, 2026, at 11:59:59 p.m. PDT (the “ **Promotion Period** ”).
* **Where is the Pokémon GO 10**  **th**  **Anniversary Sweepstakes taking place?**

* In fifty (50) United States and Washington D.C. (together the “ **United States** ”); Canada; Mexico; the United Kingdom; metropolitan France; Spain; Denmark; the Netherlands; Germany; Japan; Taiwan; and South Korea (individually each a “ **Participating Country or Region** ” and collectively the “ **Participating Countries or Regions** ”).
* **Who is eligible to enter the Pokémon GO 10**  **th**  **Anniversary Sweepstakes?**

* To be eligible to participate in this Promotion, you must be a legal resident of one of the Participating Countries or Regions, have reached at least the age of majority in your jurisdiction, have internet access and a valid email address at the time of entry into the Promotion and at the time you are awarded any Grand Prize(each an **“Eligible Participant”** ) **.**
* **Where can I find the rules for the Pokémon GO 10th Anniversary Sweepstakes?**

* These Official Rules are available free of charge at https://pokemongo.com/contest-rules/go-memories-sweepstakes for the entire duration of the Promotion.
* **What can I win?**

* **Four Tokyo Event Grand Prizes Available:** A travel package for the winner and one guest to attend Pokémon GO Festival in Tokyo, Japan, at Bay Area, currently scheduled for Friday, May 29, 2026 through Monday, June 1, 2026. The approximate retail value (“ARV”) is up to USD $9,000 (€7,635; DKK57,000; ₩13,000,000; ¥1,393,000; CAD$12,300; NT$283,800).
* **Five Chicago Event Grand Prizes Available:** A travel package for the winner and one guest to attend Pokémon GO Festival in Chicago, Illinois, U.S., currently scheduled for Friday, June 5, 2026 through Sunday, June 7, 2026. ARV:  Up to $5,000 (€4,240; DKK31,700; ₩7,247,000; ¥774,000; CAD$6,850; NT$157,700).
* **Five Denmark Event Grand Prizes Available:** A travel package for the winner and one guest to attend Pokémon GO Festival in Copenhagen, Denmark, at Faelledparken, currently scheduled for Friday, June 12, 2026 through Sunday, June 14, 2026. ARV: Up to $8,000 (€6,787; DKK50,700; ₩11,568,000; ¥1,238,000; CAD$10,950; NT$252,000).
* **What does each Grand Prize Include?**

* Round trip economy class air transportation for winner and one  guest from a major commercial airport near winner’s residence to the applicable event city; up to four  consecutive nights of standard hotel accommodations (one room, double occupancy); two event admission tickets (one for the winner and one for the guest) valid for the applicable festival dates as determined by the Sponsor; and a meal and ground transportation per diem for both travelers for up to five days of travel, calculated at USD $250 total (or local equivalent) per day for the winner and the guest combined.

* **Can I enter without making a purchase?**

* There is no purchase or consideration required to enter and/or win a prize.
* For Mexican Participants Only: Participation may or may not involve a past or future purchase.
* **How many times can I enter? What are the submission requirements?**

* There is a limit of one entry on each available platform.
* **Can I enter with my computer, or do I need a digital device?**

* You can use whatever method you normally use to visit the social media platforms.
* **What are my odds of winning a prize?**

* The odds of winning a Grand Prize depend on the total number of eligible entries received for each Prize Draw from all Participating Countries or Regions.
* **When and how will winners be selected?**

* The Promotion Period includes three separate prize draws carried out on three different dates during the Promotion Period (each a “ **Prize Draw** ”) as follows: Entry for the Tokyo Event (May 29, 2026 to June 1, 2026) ends March 22, 2026 at 11:59:59 p.m. PDT (“ **Prize Draw #1** ”); Entry for the Chicago Event (June 5, 2026 to June 7, 2026) ends April 5, 2026 at 11:59:59 p.m. PDT (“ **Prize Draw #2** ”); and entry for the Copenhagen Event (June 12, 2026 to June 14, 2026) ends April 30, 2026, at 11:59:59 p.m. PDT (“ **Prize Draw #3”** ). Entry into each Prize Draw starts March 10, 2026, at 12:00:00 a.m. PDT.
* **How many drawings will my entry be included in?**

* If you enter prior to 22 March 2026 you will be entered in Draw #1. If you are not selected as a winner in that draw, your entry will remain eligible in Prize Draw #2. If you are not selected as a winner in that draw, your entry will remain eligible in Prize Draw #3.
* **When and how will potential winners be notified?**

* **Direct Message Notification** . Potential Prize Winners will first be contacted via direct message through the applicable Entry Platform they used to enter. Any such Potential Prize Winner will need to respond to direct message with the requested information in 24-48 hours (depending on the Entry Platform rules). If a Potential Prize Winner does not have their settings on “Public” and does not respond with their requested contact information in the time provided, that Potential Prize Winner will be disqualified and an alternate prize winner may be selected, in the sole discretion of Sponsor, time-permitting. If a Potential Prize Winner timely responds with their contact information, the verification process will continue. Entrants are encouraged to check, during the Promotion Period, any archived messages on their accounts on the applicable Entry Platform in the event that any message from the Administrator is diverted to the message “request” section of their account.
* **Winner Notification/Documents a potential winner need to complete?**

* To the extent permitted by applicable law, Potential Prize Winners will then receive Grand Prize claim documents (Declaration of Eligibility, a Liability Release, travel release and, where lawful, a publicity release and any other documents required by the Sponsor (“ **Prize Claim Documents** ”) provided via DocuSign. Potential Prize Winners will also receive appropriate Forms.  All Prize Claim Documents must be executed and returned via DocuSign within five business days from the time they are received by the Potential Prize Winner, or the applicable prize may be forfeited. Prize Clam Documents will also include appropriate releases for the guest of the Potential Prize Winner. Potential Prize Winners may be required to furnish proof of identification acceptable to the Sponsor. Such proof may include that Potential Prize Winner provide proof of a valid passport and any visa or other paperwork required to travel to the winning destination. Only necessary passport details will be processed and any national identification numbers may be redacted where not legally required. The Grand Prizes will be awarded within 4-6 weeks after verification of the Potential Prize Winners’ eligibility and completion of the prize claim process.
