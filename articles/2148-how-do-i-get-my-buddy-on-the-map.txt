title: How do I get my buddy on the Map?
url: https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/2148-how-do-i-get-my-buddy-on-the-map/
---

Your buddy will only join you on the Map when its hunger meter is full. To feed your buddy, go to the Buddy page and tap “Play with Buddy.” You will then be able to feed your buddy Berries and [Poffins](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/2147-what-is-a-poffin/?p=web&s=buddy-pokemon&f=what-is-a-poffin) in AR or Quick Treat mode. Feed your buddy until their hunger meter fills up, then your buddy will appear on the Map alongside you.
