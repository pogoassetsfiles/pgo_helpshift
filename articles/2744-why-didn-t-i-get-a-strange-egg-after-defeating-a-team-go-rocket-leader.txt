To [receive a Strange Egg after defeating a Team GO Rocket Leader](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/2743-what-are-strange-eggs-and-how-do-i-obtain-them/), you’ll need to have space in your Egg inventory, Item Bag, and Pokémon storage before your battle with Sierra, Cliff, or Arlo.
