**Issue Description:** An error occurs that prevents signing in to Pokémon HOME from Pokémon GO.

**Issue Status:**  Investigating. In the meantime, please try the troubleshooting steps below. Please note that the troubleshooting steps may or may not solve the issue.

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

* Sign out from https://accounts.nintendo.com/portal on all applicable devices, including Nintendo Switch, mobile device (all browsers), and personal computer.
* Uninstall Pokémon GO and Pokémon HOME on your mobile device.
* Reinstall both apps on your mobile device.
* Open Pokémon HOME and log into your Nintendo account: Pokémon GO > Log into your account > Main Menu > Settings > Connected Devices and Services > Pokémon HOME > “Sign in with Nintendo”.
