Follow these steps to check your ticket details and Order ID:

(1) From the Confirmation Email

You can find your ticket details and Order ID in the confirmation email, which is sent from  **[[email protected]](https://niantic.helpshift.com/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection).**

Refer to the example below:

* ① Order number: Your Order ID (starts with “PGO”)
* ② Date/Time: Your ticketed event date and time

![image](../data/images/aef8b03c-3524-41e2-9b29-8c0afeaa6790.png)

(2) From the In-Game Menu

* From the Map View, tap the Main Menu
![image](../data/images/aeb56bb1-c1b6-4d6b-9190-faaea87833d5.png)
 at the bottom center of your screen.
* Tap the Events button
![image](../data/images/d536ffda-63d1-412f-8612-fd9b327fb852.png)
 located in the top-right corner of the menu.
* Find the event you are attending (e.g., Pokémon GO Fest 2026: Tokyo) and tap on the event banner.
* You can view your ticket details here, such as your selected event date and time.
* For order-related information, including your order ID, please check “View my order(s)” at the bottom of the page.
