title: What is the Lucky Trinket?

---

The  **Lucky Trinket**  is a one-time-use item that can be used to turn one friend from your Friend List into a [Lucky Friend](https://niantic.helpshift.com/faq/1485-lucky-friends/). When used, the Lucky Trinket will set the status of you and your chosen friend to Lucky, even if you aren’t Best Friends!

[![](../data/images/8f25e43c-c90a-48da-96ec-27551e080765.png)](https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/8f25e43c-c90a-48da-96ec-27551e080765.png)

The Lucky Trinket will only stay in your Item Bag for a limited time, so don’t forget to use it!

You must be at least Great Friends with the Trainer you wish to apply your Lucky Trinket to. You cannot use a Lucky Trinket with a Trainer you are currently Lucky Friends with. Using a Lucky Trinket on a friend does not change the distance at which you can complete a trade.

If you do not want to allow other Trainers to use their Lucky Trinkets on you, you can disable this permission in the Friends section of your General Settings page.
