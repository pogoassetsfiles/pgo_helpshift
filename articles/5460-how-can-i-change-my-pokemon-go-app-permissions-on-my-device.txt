If you would like to check on and change your Pokémon GO app permissions, read the steps below to find out how to access these settings.

**Please note** : It is recommended to enable certain app permissions such as Camera and Location to experience the full gameplay of Pokémon GO.

For Android:

* Go to your device’s Settings
* Scroll down and tap Apps
* Find Pokémon GO in the Apps list and tap it
* Tap Permissions
* Tap the App Permission that you wish to change

![image](../data/images/819aa83c-6ca6-44e0-b23b-94b42408432a.png)

For iOS:

* Go to your device’s Settings
* Scroll down and tap Apps
* Find Pokémon GO in the Apps list and tap it
* Tap the App Permission that you would like to change
