**Issue description:** After collecting a Stamp Rally stamp, exiting the Pokémon reward encounter without catching the Pokémon will make it appear as though the Pokémon encounter is gone.

**Workaround:** Tapping into any Stamp Rally location will trigger the Pokémon encounter.

**Issue status:** Investigating.
