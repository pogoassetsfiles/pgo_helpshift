Our main priority with Pokémon GO is to provide a fair, fun, and safe experience for all players. With that goal in mind, we take action against players who violate the [Pokémon GO Terms of Service](https://www.nianticlabs.com/terms/pokemongo/en) and the Trainer Guidelines.

This includes, but is not limited to:

* promoting or engaging in harassment against any individual or group,
* falsifying location,
* using emulators, modified or unofficial software,
* and/or accessing Pokémon GO clients or backends in an unauthorized manner including through the use of third party software or add-ons.

If you believe your account has been terminated in error incorrectly, you may [appeal the termination](https://niantic.helpshift.com/a/pokemon-go/?s=general&f=submit-a-ban-appeal&p=all) through the web-form.
