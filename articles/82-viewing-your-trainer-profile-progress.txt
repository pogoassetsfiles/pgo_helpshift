Your  **Trainer Profile**  displays key information about your Trainer and shows your progress towards reaching a new level, achieving weekly walking milestones, earning medals, and more.

To access your Trainer Profile, tap your Trainer’s portrait in the bottom left corner of the Map View:

### Profile Features

[![image](../data/images/ebcbbd1c-7693-4336-b52c-ed42135bd733-2t.png)](https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/ebcbbd1c-7693-4336-b52c-ed42135bd733.png)

**1. Trainer Profile or Friend List**  - Tap to toggle between the two views

**2.** [**Niantic Profile**](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/2595-your-niantic-profile/?s=friends-gifting-trading&f=your-niantic-profile)

**3. Trainer nickname**  and your buddy’s nickname

**4. Trainer avatar**

**5. Active buddy**

**6. Current level**  and progress towards next level

**7. Level-Up Research**  - Tap to see what tasks, if any, are required to level up.

**8. Buddy history**  - Tap to see all the Pokémon you’ve ever set as your buddy.

**9. Journal**  - Displays recent activity, such as Pokémon you’ve caught and battles you’ve won.

**10. Style**  - Tap to change your avatar’s clothing and physical features.

**11. Total Activity**  for the lifetime of your account.

[![image](../data/images/9ec74d82-a737-4ac9-bbef-e82c0f5718dc-2t.png)](https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/9ec74d82-a737-4ac9-bbef-e82c0f5718dc.png)

**12. Weekly walking goals**  - Walk 5, 25, or 50 km to earn rewards at the end of the week. Walking higher distances yields greater rewards.

**13. Egg incubation progress**  - Shows incubated egg status and additional weekly statistics

[![image](../data/images/6136b39c-b3d3-4da9-985f-523f0a9502e7-2t.png)](https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/6136b39c-b3d3-4da9-985f-523f0a9502e7.png)

**14. Gyms Recently Visited**  - Tap a Gym to view statistics related to [upgrading that Gym’s Badge](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/2409-collecting-gym-badges/). Tap "List" to see all the Gyms you’ve visited and sort by various factors.

**15. Medals**  - View all the medals you can earn, as well as your progress towards [earning or upgrading each medal](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/101-leveling-up-earning-medals/).
