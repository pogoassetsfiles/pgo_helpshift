title: What is a Poffin?
url: https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/2147-what-is-a-poffin/
---

In addition to feeding your Buddy Pokémon Berries, you can also give them  **Poffins** , a special snack that fills your buddy’s hunger meter automatically and boosts their Mood to Excited. If you give your buddy a Poffin, you will automatically earn the maximum daily number of Affection hearts in the “Give your buddy a treat” category. Your buddy will also stay full, stay on the Map, and stay Excited for longer. Poffins can be purchased from the Shop using PokéCoins.
