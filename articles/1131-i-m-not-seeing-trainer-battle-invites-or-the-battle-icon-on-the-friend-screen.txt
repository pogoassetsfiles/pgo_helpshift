If you are not receiving Trainer Battle invites or you cannot see a battle icon on your friend’s profile, check the following settings:

* Visit the Settings page, tap  **General** , and tap to enable "Allow Battle Challenges with Friends". If this is unchecked, your friends will see a "Player is currently not receiving challenges" message when they try to challenge you.
* Visit your device's date and time settings and set your device clock to sync automatically with local time.
* For child accounts, parents or guardians may need to opt into enabling multiplayer features through the [Niantic Kids Parent Portal](https://parents.nianticlabs.com/) or [Pokémon Trainer Central](https://www.pokemon.com/us/pokemon-trainer-club/login) website.

If you are still having difficulty with Trainer Battles, please [contact support](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/faq/2030-how-to-contact-support/).
