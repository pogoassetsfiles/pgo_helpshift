If you want to track your progress towards certain objectives, like [hatching Eggs](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/2358-incubators-hatching-pokemon-eggs/) or [earning Buddy Candy](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/2155-buddy-adventure/), you can add a helpful widget to your device’s home screen. These widgets will allow you to see your progress towards these objectives at a glance. Additionally, tapping them will bring you to the relevant screen in Pokémon GO.

Note that these widgets work best when you have [Adventure Sync](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/3265-adventure-sync-1644001375/) enabled.

### Apple Devices

* From your Home Screen, tap an empty area until the apps jiggle.
* Tap the ‘Edit’ button in the left hand corner.
* Tap the Add Widget  [![](../data/images/7f780d17-0656-4159-8be6-59f73c976397.jpg)](https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/7f780d17-0656-4159-8be6-59f73c976397.jpg)button in the upper-left corner.
* Select Pokémon GO, then tap Add Widget.
* Tap Done.

You can also add Pokémon GO widgets to your Today View by swiping left from your home screen and following the above steps.

[Learn more](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT207122) about Widgets on your Apple Device.

### Android Devices

* On a Home screen, touch and hold an empty space.
* Tap Widget [![](../data/images/833868b9-9784-4c6a-9f04-774522f6b076.png)](https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/833868b9-9784-4c6a-9f04-774522f6b076.png)
* Tap and hold the Pokémon GO widget. You’ll get images of your Home screens.
* Slide the widget to where you want it. Lift your finger to place the widget.

[Learn more](https://support.google.com/android/answer/9450271?hl=en#zippy=%2Cadd-or-resize-a-widget) about home screen widgets for your Android Device.
