title: What is Mega Evolution?

---

Unlike [evolving your Pokémon](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/100-evolving-your-pokemon/), which is permanent,  **Mega Evolution**  temporarily transforms your Pokémon, changing their appearance, improving their performance in battle through a significant boost in stats, and offering other unique benefits. You can tell if a Pokémon is eligible for Mega Evolution if you see a “Mega Evolve” button and “Mega Energy” on their summary page.

![image](../data/images/v4ce3x00hiwmmc7mmelu_w7vrlpfrysick-110ummymku8cgxszlumxsgb9ox23mxdmmnxxsk8bqnyr6ut7mcgkdul7uazxndxjyof1djwsi6nmojfftd1x5gyzjuhmhk4o7byjy.png)

Mega Evolving a Pokémon may also [change their type](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/2132-type-effectiveness-in-battle/). For example, Mega Charizard X is a Fire and Dragon-type Pokémon, while Charizard is a Fire and Flying-type.

**Note:** Trainers can only have one Mega-Evolved Pokémon active at a time. Also, a Pokémon’s Fast and Charged Attacks don’t change when they’re Mega-Evolved.

The more often you Mega Evolve a specific Pokémon, the higher its  **Mega Level**  will be. Mega Levels are a special type of bonus that is only active while a Pokémon is Mega-Evolved. These bonuses can affect your gameplay in a variety of ways, including increasing XP catch bonuses for Pokémon of the same type as the Mega-Evolved Pokémon.

### Viewing the Mega Pokedex

Tap the button in the bottom right of the Pokédex and select Mega to view all the Mega-Evolved Pokémon you’ve seen and caught.

![image](../data/images/ndxvueb7kv2wh4tp8wrgytemwdxsxz8nqnpwaamyw76q7znxruavkef082w55a0g9upoabtemkdbs8tmp6vhwnbz93zfxu8oxnnvb9xyc97zvgqslsulowtr9awajf9h_hq5wtzz.png)

**Note:**  Mega-Evolved Pokémon are only marked as caught once you’ve Mega-Evolved them, not when you catch a Pokémon after a Mega Raid Battle. When you defeat a Mega Raid Boss, they turn back into their regular form before you encounter and catch them, so their Mega form only be marked as seen.
