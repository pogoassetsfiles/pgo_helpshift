id: 133
title: My device is not compatible with Pokémon GO
url: https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/133-my-device-is-not-compatible-with-pokemon-go/
---

You cannot play Pokémon GO on this device because:

* You're attempting to use an unofficial or out of date version of the app. Install the latest official version from the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nianticlabs.pokemongo) or the [App Store](https://itunes.apple.com/app/pokemon-go/id1094591345), and/or

* Your device's operating system is not supported. Please [see here](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/92-supported-devices/?l=en&p=all&s=troubleshooting&f=supported-devices) for supported OS versions.
