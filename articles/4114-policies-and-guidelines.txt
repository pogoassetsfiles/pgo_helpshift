Our goal is to provide our players with a fair, fun and safe gaming experience. To do this, we maintain policies and guidelines that govern content, conduct, and behavior in our games, products, and at live events:

* [Niantic Player Guidelines](https://nianticlabs.com/guidelines?hl=en)
* [Niantic Terms of Service](https://nianticlabs.com/terms?hl=en)
* [Niantic Live Event Code of Conduct](https://nianticlabs.com/live-event-coc?hl=en)

Please note that violations of these policies and guidelines may lead to enforcement action taken against your account, including suspension or account bans.
