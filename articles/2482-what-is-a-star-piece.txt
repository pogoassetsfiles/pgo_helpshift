title: What is a Star Piece?
url: https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/2482-what-is-a-star-piece/
---

**Star Pieces**  increase the amount of Stardust you earn by 50% for a limited period of time. Star Pieces typically last for 30 minutes but may last longer during special events. [Keep an eye on the Today View](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/2852-the-today-view-1616435549/) to see what in-game events are in progress.

[![image](../data/images/ecca840c-a1f6-4893-97dc-f3702c4667d5-2t.jpg)](https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/ecca840c-a1f6-4893-97dc-f3702c4667d5.jpg)

You can also activate up to 200 Star Pieces at a time to stack the duration of their effects instead of using one every 30 minutes.
