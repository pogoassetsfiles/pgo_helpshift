Players who’ve reached level 35 can suggest improvements to an in-game location’s title, description, real-world location, or photo directly from the Pokémon GO app.*

### **To Suggest a Title, Description, or Location Improvement:**

* **Tap a PokéStop or Gym**  from the Map View.
* **Tap the arrow** in the top right corner of the Photo Disc screen.
* **Tap the three dots**  in the top right of the PokéStop/Gym details screen.
* Select from the available options.
* Complete the next screen by entering the correct title or description, or when editing location, tap and drag the map until the marker is in the correct location.

### **To Suggest a Photo:**

* Tap ADD PHOTO.
* Select TAKE PHOTO to take a photo using your device’s camera or EXISTING PHOTO to select a photo from your device’s storage.
* Once you’ve selected your photo, tap SUBMIT to submit your candidate photo for review.

**Note** : While the image may appear cropped on the confirmation screen, submitted photos do retain their original aspect ratio, either Portrait or Landscape.

### **To Report an Invalid Location**

* Select REPORT INVALID POKÉSTOP / GYM
* Select the reason you’re reporting the PokéStop or Gym from the list of potential reasons.

**Note** : If you or someone you know believe an in-game location should be removed from Pokémon GO, you can also use this webform to submit a removal request.
