### **What is the Mega Mewtwo Super Mega Raid**

Pokémon GO Fest 2026 in-person attendees will have the opportunity to participate in a Super Mega Raid featuring Mega Mewtwo during the final 30 minutes of their ticketed park gameplay session.

These Super Mega Raids will be a special kind of Raid called Unity Raids, where Trainers work together to challenge the powerful Mega Mewtwo alongside thousands of other Trainers.

To help ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all attendees, participation in this raid experience will differ from standard Raid Battles. Trainers will participate from designated areas and Gyms assigned in advance as part of the event experience.

Additional details, including participation instructions and how to confirm your designated area, will be shared with Trainers holding park experience tickets via in-game News and the email address associated with their ticket purchase.

Learn more about Super Mega Raids [here](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/faq/3330-how-can-i-join-mega-raid-battles-1771549842/#what-are-super-mega-raids?).

### **How do I join the Super Mega Raid?**

For  **Pokémon**  **GO Fest 2026** in-person event ticket holders:

* All Trainers will be assigned to a  **designated Park area** for the Super Mega Raid experience.
* You will receive  **in-game news**  informing you which area you should go to. Please make sure to check your in-game notifications.
* From the beginning of your Park session until the final 30 minutes, special Gyms marked with a star icon icon will appear only in your assigned area and only for your designated Gym assignment.

![image](../data/images/29288baa-ecc6-43a6-a0ff-0fe9cf81944b.png)

* Note that you will only be able to participate in Mega Mewtwo Super Mega Raid Battles at eligible Gyms during the final 30 minutes of your ticketed park play session.
* Tap these Gyms to join the Super Mega Raid battle against Mega Mewtwo alongside thousands of other Trainers.

### **Super Mega Raid Rewards**

Trainers who defeat Mega Mewtwo will have the opportunity to catch a special Mewtwo featuring:

* A  **Pokémon GO Fest 2026 Location Background**
* **Mega Level 1 unlocked** *

*If you’re lucky, it might even be at Mega Level 2 or 3!

### **Additional Opportunities to Encounter Mega Mewtwo**

If you are unable to participate in the Super Mega Raid, GO Fest in-person event ticket holders will also have another opportunity to encounter Mega Mewtwo through  **City Experience Timed Research** .

For more details, please visit the official announcement:
https://pokemongo.com/en/news/mega-mewtwo-gofest-2026

### **FAQ**

Q: How can I check my designated Super Mega Raid Gym assignment?

A: We have sent both an in-game notification and an email to the address registered with your ticket purchase regarding your assigned area for the “Super Mega Raid” taking place at the Pokémon GO Fest 2026 in-person park venues.

To confirm your assigned Super Mega Raid area and eligible Gyms, please check your in-game News notification, as this is the most reliable way to view your assignment.

* Tap the  **Poké Ball**  on the map screen.
* Open  **News**  from the bottom-right menu.
* Open the notification:
 **“Your Pokémon GO Fest 2026: [in-person event location] end-of-session Super Mega Raid assignment”**
* Your assigned  **Super Mega Raid area and eligible Gyms**  will be shown in the message.

![image](../data/images/45486baf-1f2d-48c6-a9c6-3e4d2e833fa6.png)

Q: Can I change my designated raid area?

A: The assigned raid area shown in the in-game news and event experience is final.To help ensure a smooth play experience for all Trainers during the Mewtwo raid event, we’re unable to accommodate requests to change assigned raid areas. This includes at the Technical Support Tent at in-person park venues or through in-app support.
