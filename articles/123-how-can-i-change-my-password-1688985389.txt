title: How can I change my password?

Pokémon GO does not store account passwords. Your account password is maintained by the login provider you use to log into the game. To change or reset your password, please use the resources listed below:

* [Google](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/41078?source=gsearch&hl=en)
* [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/help/213395615347144)
* [Apple](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201487)
* [Niantic Kids](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/18-niantic-kids/faq/1430-reset-my-password/?p=web&s=troubleshooting&f=reset-my-password)
* [Nintendo](https://en-americas-support.nintendo.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/15991)
* [Pokémon Company](https://recover.pokemon.com/?login_challenge=f30a7c1bea174cd49d69ee7485ea30ac&lng=)

Be sure to check your spam or junk folder if you don't receive the password reset email promptly.
