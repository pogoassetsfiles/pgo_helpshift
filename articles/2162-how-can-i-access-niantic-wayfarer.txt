Features related to Niantic Wayfarer are accessible for certain experienced players in Pokémon GO. Whether you can submit a suggested Wayspot edit, submit a Wayspot nomination, or review nominations and edits is determined by the level of your account in Pokémon GO. Current access requirements are listed below.

**Please note**  that these access requirements are subject to change, so keep an eye on our official channels for updates.

### Submitting Wayspot Nominations

Players can nominate new Wayspots for review and manage their nominations on the [Wayfarer website](https://wayfarer.nianticlabs.com/):

* Level 35 in Pokémon GO - Learn how to submit a Pokéstop [here](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/41-submitting-a-pokestop-nomination/).

### Submitting Wayspot Edits

Players can submit suggested edits to existing Wayspots for review within Wayfarer:

* Level 35 in Pokémon GO - Learn how to submit a Pokéstop edit [here](https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/2144-submitting-a-pokestop-or-gym-contribution/?p=web&s=pokestops&f=submitting-a-pokestop-nomination).

### Reviewing Submitted Wayspot Nominations and Edits

Players can visit the [Wayfarer website](https://wayfarer.nianticlabs.com/) to review Wayspot nominations and edits. In addition to level requirements, players must also complete the Wayfarer onboarding to show that they understand Niantic’s Wayspot acceptance criteria.

* Level 35 in Pokémon GO
