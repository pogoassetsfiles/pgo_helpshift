### iOS

* Open the  **App Store**  app.

[![image](../data/images/04f757dd-11cc-4208-9a05-c226691a20cf-2t.png)](https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/04f757dd-11cc-4208-9a05-c226691a20cf.png)
* Tap  **Today** .

[![image](../data/images/e451f291-0730-4053-a439-b78baca29adc-2t.jpg)](https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/e451f291-0730-4053-a439-b78baca29adc.jpg)
* Tap the  **Profile**  button at the top of the screen.

[![image](../data/images/9a9c5955-5ae2-438b-83a8-09fa33d0aae7-2t.jpg)](https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/9a9c5955-5ae2-438b-83a8-09fa33d0aae7.jpg)
* Navigate to the  **Pokémon GO**  app and tap the  **Update**  button.

### Android

* Open the  **Google Play Store**  app.

[![image](../data/images/71b6650e-5ae9-4d81-8693-aefed5165591-2t.png)](https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/71b6650e-5ae9-4d81-8693-aefed5165591.png)
* On the top right, tap your  **profile photo** .

[![](../data/images/261d3dc0-dc57-415d-943d-4c1c8daff06f.png)](https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/261d3dc0-dc57-415d-943d-4c1c8daff06f.png)
* Tap the  **Manage apps & device** menu option.

https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/08c38a91-1e58-475a-8517-fab5142e400e.png[![](../data/images/714a2edf-8096-460c-bf71-b214c3c5f14d.png)](https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/714a2edf-8096-460c-bf71-b214c3c5f14d.png)https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/08c38a91-1e58-475a-8517-fab5142e400e.png
* Check if you have any pending updates from the  **Overview**  tab.

https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/cf59b26a-31c8-4ab3-9af8-c8e047495a40.png[![](../data/images/358b6cf9-088d-46fc-9fc3-42eeb740e9dc.png)](https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/358b6cf9-088d-46fc-9fc3-42eeb740e9dc.png) [![](../data/images/3071caea-62ea-4bb6-8e06-c5512084f103.png)](https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/3071caea-62ea-4bb6-8e06-c5512084f103.png)https://d2duuy9yo5pldo.cloudfront.net/niantic/cf59b26a-31c8-4ab3-9af8-c8e047495a40.png
* To double check, search  **Pokémon GO**  from the Play Store main screen and see if there are any updates available.
