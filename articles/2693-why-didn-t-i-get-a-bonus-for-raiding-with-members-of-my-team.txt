title: Why didn’t I get a bonus for raiding with members of my team?
url: https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/2693-why-didn-t-i-get-a-bonus-for-raiding-with-members-of-my-team/
---

As of the release of Mega Evolution in Pokémon GO, the Premier Ball bonus for completing a Raid Battle with team members has been replaced by a speed bonus. Defeat Raid Bosses as fast as you can to receive extra Premier Balls for the encounter in a regular Raid Battle or extra Mega Energy in a Mega Raid Battle.
