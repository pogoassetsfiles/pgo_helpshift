On Android, you can restrict in-app purchases by requiring a password for each in-app purchase:

* Open the  **Google Play Store**  app.
* Tap  **Menu**  >  **Settings**  >  **Require Authentication For Purchases** .
* Select the  **For all purchases through Google Play on this device**  option *.*
