If you’ve been suspended for violating the Player Guidelines while participating in Routes, click on the “ **Routes Appeal”**  button down below and fill out the corresponding form.

Please note that this form is specifically designed for appealing a game suspension incurred as a result of inappropriate behavior or misconduct while participating in Routes. Do not use this form to contest a rejected Route submission.
