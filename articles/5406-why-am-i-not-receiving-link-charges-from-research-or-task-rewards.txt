title: Why am I not receiving Link Charges from Research or task rewards?
url: https://niantic.helpshift.com/hc/en/6-pokemon-go/faq/5406-why-am-i-not-receiving-link-charges-from-research-or-task-rewards/
---

Trainers can only hold up to 600 Link Charges at a time. If you are close to or have reached this limit, you will not be able to receive any amount of Link Charges that would exceed it.

Please spend enough Link Charges to free up enough space before claiming additional rewards.
